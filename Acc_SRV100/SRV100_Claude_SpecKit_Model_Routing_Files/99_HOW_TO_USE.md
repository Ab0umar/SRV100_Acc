# How To Use

## Step 1
Open Claude.

## Step 2
Paste the content of:
`03_CLAUDE_MASTER_PROMPT.md`

## Step 3
Ask Claude:
`Generate /specify, /plan, and /tasks for this project.`

## Step 4
Claude will generate tasks with model routing.

## Step 5
Take each generated task and run it in its assigned tool:
- Cursor
- Codex
- GPT
- Gemini
- GLM/Kimi
- Ollama/Continue

## Step 6
After execution, return results to Claude for `/review`.

## Important
Claude is the brain.
Other tools are the hands.
